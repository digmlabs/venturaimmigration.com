About Us

Home Our Firm

We are an immigration consultancy located in Delta BC. We provide substantive support to people who want to come to Canada as students, workers, travel, or business purposes. We help with immigration and citizenship needs by following immigration procedures and any related legislation. Expert client advice and guidance is delivered on how to obtain proper documentation and processing of applications. We develop a start to finish plan for your immigration needs and with the help of our team we try to deliver successful and timely results.

The company was started by Director Mr. Lovedeep Singh in 2012. His vision is to be an effective regulator of the business as an authorized member of CICC (The College of Immigration and Citizenship Consultants) with an effort to deliver within the policies and regulations of immigration laws, at the same time, protecting immigration service consumers by providing them transparency while they avail to the services of the company.

Lovedeep Singh is the founder and managing director of the company. He is certified CICC (The College of Immigration and Citizenship Consultants) Member with good standing. He has been working in the industry since 2008, over 12 years of extensive experience up his sleeve. He has been dealing with prestigious educational institutions and reputed businesses in the community. Lovedeep came to Canada as an international student in 2003 and after completing his education he worked hard to get his PR. His journey from starting as a student and getting PR made him choose a profession in immigration so he could help students and temporary residents to get their PR. His problem solving and forecasting skills have helped him built a good clientele over the years. He has helped thousands of immigrants in attaining PR of Canada.

Our firm strives to provide great customer experiences. Our team is reliable and aware of the Canadian Immigration system and therefore we help our clients to achieve their long-term goals with honest advice. We believe that every individual has a unique situation when it comes to immigration needs, so we thoroughly access and review their situation before we personalize a process for them.
