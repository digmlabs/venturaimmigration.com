Provincial Nominee Programs

Each province has its own streams and requirements set to target temporary residents to contribute to their economy. The application differs based on the stream you are applying to. The application process could be paper-based or online and regardless of where you plan to reside in Canada you need to pass a medical exam and police clearance certificate.

The process could be complicated but if you want to stay in one of the provinces of Canada it's best to have us represent you in filing for your provincial nominee programs to help you get the privileges of being a Canadian while living in the most beautiful places in Canada.

Express Entry (CEC, FSWP, FSTP)

This is an online system to submit applications for permanent residents for those who receive an invitation to apply. You need to be eligible to submit your profile in this category. We help you manage your application in a timely manner as this process is fast with proper documentation and procedural standards.

Appeals

The Immigration Appeal Division (IAD) hears appeals on immigration- related matters. This is inclusive of residency obligations, removal orders, sponsorships etc. There are procedures and guidelines outlined for this process, involving in depth knowledge about Canadian immigration and law. There are practice notices applicable to refugee claims and immigration matters, detention reviews and admissibility hearings.

We as consultants could help you in filing for appeals, depending on individual situations. There are legal concepts involved and it is highly suggestive in such cases to hire a third party to act on your behalf to make your process simpler and more efficient.

Business/Investment Immigration

Canada offers investment-based immigration programs. Business immigrants can start up or invest in a business to support the Canadian economy. The immigrants can enter with a start-up visa or could be self-employed persons. Canadian model, business immigrants can avail to a national health care program, affordable quality education and a national pension system that provides quantifiable annual income upon retirement. The goal is to initiate businesses with potential.

Labour Market Impact Assessment (LMIA)

Employers in Canada need this document to hire foreign workers in Canada. When there is a shortage of Canadian workers available to do a job, foreign workers can be hired to fill labour and skill shortages. This application process depends on the type of program used to hire.
Ask us how you can get an LMIA or hire through the International Mobility Program which exempts you from having an LMIA. We can also assist in hiring Francophone or bilingual temporary foreign workers from outside Quebec.

Humanitarian and Compassion Considerations

Super Visa and Parents Grandparents PR

Super Visa is a multiple entry visa issued for a period of 10 years for parents and grandparents only, and what makes it different from a 10-year multiple entry visa is that it allows an individual owning it to stay in Canada for up to 2years at a time. However, there are certain requirements to do so. You can also sponsor your parents and grandparents to Canada on a permanent basis. We can help you file your sponsorship application and work out alternative ways for you to reunite with your family members.
